MargDarshak : From Blank Page to Blueprint

MargDarshak is a comprehensive AI-driven program design platform that transforms how education NGOs conceptualize, structure, and launch impactful programs. By combining expert-aligned frameworks, contextual intelligence, and gamification, MargDarshak eliminates fragmented, consultant-heavy program design processes and enables organizations to independently build high-quality, funder-ready program blueprints.

Problem Statement and Market Need
Education NGOs face deep structural challenges in designing effective programs before implementation:

High Design Inefficiency: 50–60% of program planning time is spent on unstructured discussions, rework, and misalignment.

Expert Dependency: NGOs rely heavily on external consultants, costing ₹5–20 lakhs per program design cycle.

Inconsistent Program Quality: Lack of standardized design frameworks leads to uneven outcomes across regions and organizations.

Slow Design-to-Launch: Program conceptualization to approval takes 3–6 months, delaying implementation.

Weak Impact Linkage: Daily activities are often disconnected from student outcomes and systemic change.

Scaling Barriers: Programs fail to scale due to unclear logic, poor measurement frameworks, and weak documentation.

This creates a strong sector-wide need for a guided, scalable, and intelligence-driven program design solution.

Solution Overview – How MargDarshak Works MargDarshak is an AI-powered, gamified program design pipeline that guides NGOs from a blank page to a complete program blueprint.

Intelligent Onboarding Layer: Captures organization context (geography, theme, scale, maturity) and auto-aligns design pathways.

AI Design Intelligence Engine: Assists with problem definition, outcome setting, logic validation, and indicator selection.

Gamified Workflow Engine: Breaks complex program design into simple, step-by-step tasks with progress tracking.

Context-Aware Logic Modeling: Ensures coherence between problems, interventions, stakeholders, and impact.

Multi-Output Generation: Produces Logical Frameworks, Theory of Change diagrams, indicators, and reports automatically.

Comprehensive Feature Set – MargDarshak Intelligent onboarding & context setup: Captures NGO context (geography, theme, scale) to personalize the entire program design journey.

AI-assisted problem definition & refinement: Helps NGOs articulate clear, evidence-based problems and flags vagueness or solution bias.

Student outcome designer (SMART & measurable): Guides creation of age-appropriate, SMART learning outcomes aligned to NEP and curricula.

Gamified Theory of Change builder: Breaks complex logic models into simple, step-by-step cause-to-impact pathways.

Stakeholder & practice mapping: Maps key system actors and defines required practice changes for each stakeholder.

Indicator & measurement framework: Auto-suggests meaningful indicators with baselines, targets, and timelines.

Quality checks & completeness scoring: Evaluates design readiness and highlights missing or weak program components.

Multilingual support (English + Hindi): Enables inclusive program design through seamless language switching.

Export-ready LFAs, diagrams, and reports: Instantly generates funder-ready documents, visuals, and planning templates.

Technology Stack – MargDarshak Backend Framework: FastAPI (Python)

Database: MongoDB Atlas (flexible, cloud-native)

AI & Logic: Rule-based + heuristic intelligence engines

Visualization: NetworkX + Matplotlib (Theory of Change diagrams)

Exports: ReportLab (PDF), OpenPyXL (Excel), python-pptx (PPT)

Multilingual Support: Middleware-based translation (English + Hindi)

Deployment: Render (backend), Cloud-ready architecture

Frontend: React (dashboard & workflow UI)
